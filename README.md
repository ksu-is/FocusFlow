# FocusFlow

FocusFlow is a desktop app concept for students who need to stay productive through long study sessions **without burning out**.  
The goal is to combine focus timing, break structure, and simple progress tracking in one lightweight experience.

## Why FocusFlow

Many students either:
- push too hard and lose consistency, or
- take breaks without structure and lose momentum.

FocusFlow is intended to help with both by encouraging a sustainable rhythm:
1. Focus in short, intentional blocks.
2. Take planned recovery breaks.
3. Track progress over a full session.

## Planned Core Experience

The app is intended to support:
- **Configurable focus/break cycles** (Pomodoro-style and custom lengths)
- **Session planning** (set a study goal before starting)
- **Progress visibility** (time completed, cycles finished, streaks)
- **Light reminders** for break starts/ends
- **Low-distraction interface** designed for deep work

## Example Session Flow

1. Choose your subject and session goal.
2. Start a focus timer.
3. Take prompted short breaks.
4. Repeat cycles until session completion.
5. Review a quick end-of-session summary.
